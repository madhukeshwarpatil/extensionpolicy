ClearNow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2024

Summary: ClearNow does not collect, transmit, or share any user data with external servers. All data is stored locally in your browser and never leaves your device.

1. Data Collection

ClearNow does not collect any personal information or browsing data. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser and does not transmit any data to external servers, third-party services, or analytics platforms.

2. Local Data Storage

ClearNow stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

  • User Preferences: Your theme preference (light/dark mode), auto-reload setting, domain profile settings, and popup reminder preferences
  • Clearing History: A local log of cache clearing operations including:
    • Domain names where cache was cleared
    • Timestamp of each clearing operation
    • Options selected (cache, cookies, localStorage, etc.)
    • Number of items cleared
  • Last Cleared Timestamps: Per-domain timestamps tracking when cache was last cleared (used for badge reminders)

All this data is stored in Chrome's local storage and sync storage, which is encrypted and managed by Chrome itself. This data never leaves your device.

3. Permissions Explanation

ClearNow requires the following permissions to function:

browsingData: Required to clear browser cache, cookies, history, and downloads. This permission allows the extension to remove browsing data from your browser.
storage: Used to save your preferences and clearing history locally in your browser. No data is transmitted externally.
tabs: Required to access information about open tabs and clear storage across multiple tabs when needed.
scripting: Required to inject scripts into web pages to clear localStorage, sessionStorage, and IndexedDB data.
activeTab: Required to access information about the currently active tab (domain, URL, etc.).
Host Permissions (<all_urls>): Required to:
  • Show on-screen reminders on development and QA/staging websites
  • Detect the environment type (dev/QA/production) of websites
  • Extract website primary colors for dynamic theming

Important: The extension does not access or read any content from websites. It only injects scripts to clear storage data when you explicitly request it, or shows reminders on dev/QA sites (if enabled).

4. Data Transmission

ClearNow does not transmit any data to external servers. The extension:

  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not send data to third-party APIs
  • Does not collect usage statistics
  • Does not communicate with external servers
  • All operations are performed locally in your browser

5. Third-Party Services

ClearNow does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs. The extension is completely self-contained and operates entirely within your browser.

6. User Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View History: You can view your clearing history in the extension popup
  • Export Data: You can export your clearing history as JSON files
  • Clear History: You can delete all clearing history at any time
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data
  • Preferences: You can change or reset your preferences at any time

7. Data Security

All data stored by ClearNow is managed by Chrome's built-in storage system, which:

  • Is encrypted by Chrome
  • Is stored locally on your device
  • Is protected by Chrome's security model
  • Is not accessible to other extensions or websites

8. Children's Privacy

ClearNow does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the extension does not collect any data, this is not applicable.
